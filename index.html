About Me

I am a Florida International University graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering. I am currently employed at Collins Aerospace, a subdivision of Raytheon Technologies(RTX), working on the fast-paced Repair and Overhaul of commercial and military aircraft landing gear division. As an engineer, I focus on process improvement by tracking Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and working alongside a team to develop robust, mistake-proof, solutions to reduce defects, re-work, and improve delivery times while satisfying customer requirements and industry standards.


I am currently enrolled in the University of Miami Coding Bootcamp where I am learning new languages and tools that will enhance my problem-solving skills to become a Full Stack developer. I am eager to learn about Responsive Design, Bootstrap, Handle Bars, MySQL, MERN Stack, AJAX, and many more tools this boot camp offers. Once I am done with this Bootcamp my plan is to continue furthering my education in Data Analysis or Cyber Security.


As a South Florida resident, I am fluent in Spanish and enjoy the outdoor activities our state has to offer such as cycling, hiking, and fishing. When the weather is not permitting, I enjoy staying inside and working on home improvement projects ranging from something physical like remodeling a bathroom to something technical like building a Plex Media home server I am always open to new ideas and willing to learn new skills.
